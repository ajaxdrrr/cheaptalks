Our Story

Welcome to Cheaptalks, We are a group of passionate technologists who came together with a common vision of delivering innovative and cutting-edge solutions to our clients. Our story began in the early 2019, when our founders, John and Sarah, both computer science graduates, realized the potential of technology to transform businesses.

Starting from a small room in their apartment, they worked tirelessly to create software solutions for small and medium-sized businesses. Their hard work paid off, and soon their reputation for quality and reliability grew. With a growing number of clients and a team of talented developers.

Over the years, our company has expanded its services to include a wide range of IT solutions, including software development, mobile app development, web design, and digital marketing. Our team comprises experienced developers, designers, project managers, and digital marketers who are committed to delivering exceptional value to our clients.

We have worked with businesses across various industries, including healthcare, finance, education, and retail. Our approach is simple yet effective – we take the time to understand our clients’ unique requirements and business goals and then develop custom solutions that are tailored to their specific needs.
Our commitment to quality has earned us numerous accolades and recognitions, including industry awards and certifications. We are proud to have a client retention rate of over 90%, a testament to the quality of our work and the level of trust that our clients have in us.

At our company, we believe that technology should be used to make life better – for businesses and individuals alike. We are committed to staying at the forefront of technology and delivering innovative solutions that help our clients achieve their business objectives.
Thank you for considering Cheaptalks for your IT needs. We look forward to the opportunity to work with you and help you achieve your goals.
